About OnWare

Onware is an E-commerce website developed using Django(backend).We sell automobile parts on this website. The main objective of this project is to provide automobile parts at a cheap price to the customer. Here customers can buy automobile parts directly from sellers at a very low cost.

We act as a mediator between small scale manufacturers and the customer. We provide B2B[to retailers] or B2C[to customers] services.

Customers receive relatively less price for purchasing automobile parts. It is beneficial to sellers as they grow their business by using Onware.
